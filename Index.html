Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 14, 2025

Anunuebe Cottage Library ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Email address (for login and account access)
  • Download activity (e.g., which books, audios, or videos you access)
  • Purchase data (for paid content via in-app purchase or donations)
  • Device info (for app performance and analytics)
  • Optional metadata from media playback or downloads

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to your library and purchases
  • Offer technical support and personalized features
  • Monitor downloads and usage (non-personally identifiable)
  • Secure the app and prevent abuse
  • Enable purchases and donations via PayPal or Google Play billing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted services that may collect or process data on our behalf:

  • Firebase (authentication, analytics, database, and storage)
  • Google AdMob (in-app advertising)
  • Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchase
  • PayPal (for donations)

These services have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted servers managed by Firebase. We use industry-standard security protocols to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Your Choices

  • You can delete your account or data by contacting us directly
  • You may opt out of personalized ads via your device settings

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
